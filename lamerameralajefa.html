Welcome to La Jefa Radio MN,

a digital space for the Hispanic community in the United States.

Our almost 30,000 followers from the US and abroad love and watch our talk shows through our digital platforms.

  • ABOUT US

    La Jefa Radio MN has been broadcasting for almost four years, bringing entertainment, fun, and joy to all our online community.

    We have invested in technology and other equipment in order to become the #1 digital platform in Minneapolis in the next years.

    Even though we are a small business, we are known as a trustworthy source of biligual content and entertainment by the community and other small and big companies in Minnesota.

  • VALUES

    Social responsibility, honesty, discipline, professionalism, joy, solidarity, working with the truth and adhering to the principles of respect and good coexistence between genders, classes, sectors, ethnic groups and sexual preferences.

  • MISSION

    To be part of our audience, of their daily joy, of their coexistence and that our programming serves not only to entertain, but also to educate, inform, motivate and guide in a healthy, fun and informal way, based on our philosophy of transmitting good news with a positive and motivating attitude in the daily life of our listeners.

  • VISION

    Reach the highest quality standards in service to our audience, with excellent sound, fidelity and offering fresh, real, interesting information, delivered with respect and objectivity.

    To be a tool for learning with cultural capsules, motivation and that the audience is part of the programming with their participation, ideas and inclusion in topics, proposals and improvements, and that this leads to the success of the radio and therefore of society.
